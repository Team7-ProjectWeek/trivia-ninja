My name is Michael Porter. I went to undergrad at UCLA and majored in Molecular Biology. I started learning to code while studying Immunology in graduate school at UCSD. I began with some intro college courses and powered through tutorials to learn the fundamentals. I've built a few things in Ruby, read a lot of blog articles and spent some time understanding backend design decisions. I moved to Seattle to work at the Dept. of Health and wanted to continue coding as a means of becoming financially independent. At the time I wasn't sure how my skills fit in to the wider world of programmers, I had never coded with anyone else and was learning a technology that was getting a little less popular. I attended Code Fellows to gain an understanding of my skills, to code with others, learn fresher technology and transition into the tech field.
